## Viewing Sales Targets

### My Sales Targets

1. **Navigate to**: HR → Sales Targets
2. **View Current Targets**:
   - See monthly targets
   - View target amount
   - Check achievement status
   - View achievement percentage

3. **Target History**:
   - View past targets
   - Compare performance across months
   - Track progress

### Team Targets

1. **Navigate to**: HR → Sales Targets → Team/Dept Targets
2. **View Team Performance**:
   - See team targets
   - View team achievement
   - Compare with individual performance

## Understanding Incentive Calculations

### Sales Incentive Calculation

1. **Achievement-Based**:
   - Incentive based on target achievement
   - Tiered structure (0-50%, 50-75%, 75-100%, 100%+)
   - Accelerator bonuses for exceeding targets

2. **Calculation Formula**:
   - Base amount × Achievement % × Incentive rate
   - Subject to minimum threshold and maximum cap
   - Accelerator rates for exceeding 100%

### Performance Incentives

1. **Individual Performance**:
   - Based on individual performance metrics
   - Calculated monthly/quarterly

2. **Team Performance**:
   - Shared team incentive
   - Distributed based on contribution

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How is my incentive calculated?**
A: Incentive is calculated based on your achievement percentage, tier structure, and incentive rates defined in the plan.

**Q: When will I receive my incentive?**
A: Incentives are typically processed with monthly salary. Check the payout status in "My Incentives".

**Q: Can I view my PF balance?**
A: Yes, navigate to "My Account" under Provident Fund to view your balance and statements.

**Q: How do I update my sales achievement?**
A: Achievement is typically updated automatically from the sales system. Contact HR if manual update is needed.
