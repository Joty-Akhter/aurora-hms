# Payroll & Benefits Administration System

## 📋 Overview

The Payroll & Benefits Administration System is a comprehensive, intelligent platform designed to streamline payroll processing, benefits management, and compensation administration. This advanced system leverages automation, AI-powered insights, and real-time processing to ensure accurate payroll calculations, efficient benefits administration, and compliance with complex tax and labor regulations while providing exceptional employee experience.

### Key Objectives
- **Automated Payroll Processing**: End-to-end payroll automation with 99.9% accuracy
- **Comprehensive Benefits Management**: Complete benefits lifecycle administration
- **Retirement Benefits Administration**: Provident Fund, 401(k), and pension plan management
- **Incentives Management**: Comprehensive incentive plan administration and payout processing
- **Tax Compliance Excellence**: Automated tax calculations and regulatory compliance
- **Employee Self-Service**: Intuitive employee portal for payroll and benefits
- **Real-Time Processing**: Instant payroll calculations and benefits updates
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Global payroll processing capabilities
- **Advanced Analytics**: Data-driven insights for compensation optimization
- **Integration Excellence**: Seamless integration with HR and financial systems

### System Capabilities
- **Payroll Processing**: Automated salary calculations, deductions, and payments
- **Benefits Administration**: Complete benefits enrollment, management, and tracking
- **Tax Management**: Automated tax calculations and compliance reporting
- **Compensation Planning**: Salary structures and compensation analysis
- **Incentives Management**: Comprehensive incentive plan management and payout processing
- **Retirement Benefits**: 401(k), pension plans, and Provident Fund administration
- **Employee Self-Service**: Payroll and benefits self-service portal
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Comprehensive payroll and benefits reporting
- **Compliance Management**: Regulatory compliance and audit support
- **Integration Platform**: Seamless system integration capabilities

## 🔹 9. Compensation Planning

> **Detailed requirements** for **salary structure**, **salary components**, and **employee salaries** are specified in **[salary_management_requirements.md](salary_management_requirements.md)**. The following is a summary; implementers must refer to that document for full requirement IDs, priorities, and integration points.

### Salary Structure (see [salary_management_requirements.md § 1](salary_management_requirements.md#1-salary-structure))
- **Grades and bands**: Salary grades with display order; bands/ranges with min, max, mid, currency; effective-from / effective-to dates.
- **Scope**: Structures scoped by organization (and optionally department/location); positions/job roles linked to default grade.
- **Lifecycle**: Audit log and versioning; no edits to structure for payroll-closed periods.
- **APIs and reports**: CRUD via UI/API; list structures by organization and effective date; structure summary report.

### Salary Components (see [salary_management_requirements.md § 2](salary_management_requirements.md#2-salary-components))
- **Master**: Code, name, type (earning/deduction), category (Basic, HRA, allowances, statutory/voluntary deductions, loans); org-scoped; effective dating; display order.
- **Calculation**: Fixed amount; percentage of basic (or other base); formula; statutory/loan reference; optional ceiling/floor and conditional applicability.
- **Statutory**: Tagging for PF wage, taxability (taxable/exempt); used by payroll and PF/tax modules.
- **Lifecycle**: Audit; deactivation without delete; no delete if used in employee salary or past payroll.
- **APIs and reports**: CRUD; list by org and type; component master report.

### Employee Salaries (see [salary_management_requirements.md § 3](salary_management_requirements.md#3-employee-salaries))
- **Assignment**: Employee assigned to salary structure and grade with effective-from / effective-to; optional inheritance from position.
- **Component values**: Per-employee amount/percentage/override per component with effective dating; support from structure/grade template; add/remove components per employee; proration rules.
- **Revisions**: New effective-dated rows for increments/promotions; revision history and optional bulk revision; no back-dated changes for closed payroll periods.
- **Payslip**: Calculate total earnings, total deductions, net pay; store or reproduce for payroll run; payslip view with all components.
- **Validation**: Active employment; no duplicate effective periods; component limits where configured.
- **APIs and reports**: View/edit via UI; APIs for current or as-of-date salary and component breakdown; employee salary list, revision history, grade-wise headcount/cost.

### Core Compensation Features (additional)
- **Salary Management** (detailed in [salary_management_requirements.md](salary_management_requirements.md)):
