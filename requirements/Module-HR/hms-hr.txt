HR Module

1. Purpose
The HR Module manages all employee-related processes, including employee data, attendance, leave, shift management, payroll, and accounting integration. It ensures accurate salary computation, compliance with hospital policies, and audit-ready reporting.

2. Master Data / Configure
2.1 Department Info
Department name and code
Department head
Mapping employees
Approval Matrix Configuration
2.2 Designation Info
Add / Edit / Delete designations
Link to department
Hierarchy level (for reporting and leave approvals)
2.3 Employee Registration
Personal info: Name, DOB, Contact, Address
Professional info: Designation, Department, Joining Date, Employee ID
Bank info for salary transfer
Work status: Active / Inactive / Resigned

3. Attendance & Shift Management
3.1 Shift Info
Shift name, timing, grace period, break time
Night / Day / Rotational shifts
3.2 Employee Roster
Individual / Department-wise roster
Monthly roster planning
Roster details, including holidays, leave, duty changes
3.3 Attendance
Automatic import from biometric / device
Manual adjustment for exceptions
Grace Time Entry for late arrivals
3.4 Duty Info Management
Assign duties to employees
Replace employee's duty in case of absence
3.5 Overtime Setting and Entry
Configure overtime rates per shift/employee category
Record overtime hours
Integration with payroll

4. Leave & Holiday Management
4.1 Employee Leave
Employee leave form submission
In-charge verification
Management approval
4.2 Holiday Management
Holiday setup per year / department
Employee-specific holiday info
4.3 Leave Deduction
Automatic leave deduction from salary
Deduction based on in/out timing or leave balance
5. Payroll & Salary Management
5.1 Salary Components
Pay register creation
Increment salary
Salary additions (allowances, incentives)
Salary deductions (loan, leave, tax, penalties)
5.2 Loan and Advance
Loan issuance to employees
Loan refund/deduction
Integration with payroll
5.3 Bonus & Incentives
Create bonus registers
Link to payroll
Staff category-wise bonuses
5.4 Payroll Processing
Create a salary per employee per month
Automatic calculation based on attendance, leave, overtime, bonuses, deductions
Generates salary slips

6. Integration & Accounting
Payroll impact on accounts:
Salary Expense Account (Debit)
Employee Loan / Payable / Bonus Account (Credit)
Leave deduction and overtime also reflected in accounting
Audit-ready ledger entries for HR transactions
